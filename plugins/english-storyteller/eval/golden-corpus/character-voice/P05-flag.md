"Movement on the north wire," Johnson said.

The sergeant looked up from his food.

"Where exactly?"

"Northeast corner. Where the rain washed out."

The sergeant set his spoon down. "How long ago?"

"Twenty minutes. Maybe thirty."

"I'll have Martinez take another look and verify what you observed before we escalate this to command," the sergeant said.

Johnson waited. The sergeant picked up his spoon again.
